About Me

My name is Wonder Karomo, and I am pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Science in Information Technology at Ensign College with a career focus in Cybersecurity and System Administration. Professional Ethics

One of the most significant case studies I examined was the 2017 Equifax Data Breach. The incident demonstrated the importance of timely software updates, vulnerability management, transparency, and ethical responsibility in cybersecurity. As a future cybersecurity professional, I believe protecting information is both a technical and moral responsibility. Organizations must maintain secure systems, communicate honestly during security incidents, and place the protection of people above convenience.
